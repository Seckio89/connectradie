## рџџЎ WARN вЂ” RLS Policy Re-evaluates Auth Per Row

**Table:** `public.imported_calendar_visits`  
**Policy:** `owner manages imported visits`

This policy calls `auth.<function>()` (or `current_setting()`) directly rather than via a sub-select, causing it to be re-evaluated for every row scanned. This is a known performance footgun at scale.

**Fix:** Replace `auth.uid()` with `(select auth.uid())` inside the policy expression.

```sql
-- Example fix (create new migration):
ALTER POLICY "owner manages imported visits" ON public.imported_calendar_visits
  USING ((select auth.uid()) = owner_id);
```

Reference: https://supabase.com/docs/guides/database/database-linter?lint=0003_auth_rls_initplan
