## 🟡 NEW — V1 / V2 Pricing Co-Existence Risk in `_shared/pricing.ts`

`_shared/pricing.ts` now contains **two complete fee models** side by side:

- **V1 (legacy):** `calculatePlatformFee()`, `calculateProcessingFee()` — sliding tiers, cap $400 (free) / $250 (pro)
- **V2 (new):** `calculatePlatformFeeCentsV2()`, `TIER_SCHEDULES` — basis-points, integer cents, cap $900 (free) / $630 (pro) / $270 (PM)

**Action:**

## 🟡 Persistent — RLS Policy Re-Evaluates Auth Per Row

**Table:** `public.imported_calendar_visits`  
**Policy:** `"owner manages imported visits"`

Calls `auth.uid()` or `current_setting()` directly (not via sub-select), causing per-row re-evaluation. At scale this becomes a meaningful performance hit.

**Fix (new migration):**
```sql
ALTER POLICY "owner manages imported visits" ON public.imported_calendar_visits
  USING ((select auth.uid()) = owner_id);
```

Reference: https://supabase.com/docs/guides/database/database-linter?lint=0003_auth_rls_initplan
